## Overview

The Ember Council is the governing body of [Ironhold](../locations/ironhold.md) and the most powerful political institution in the Ashlands. Originally formed as a wartime council of seven mages and military commanders during [The Sundering](../lore/the-sundering.md), it has since consolidated into a permanent oligarchic authority.

## Structure

The Council consists of seven seats, each representing a domain of governance: Arcana, War, Commerce, Infrastructure, Diplomacy, Justice, and the Forge. Seats are held for life and filled by Council appointment on vacancy.

Current members include:
- **[Malachar the Grey](../npcs/malachar-the-grey.md)** — Seat of Arcana
- **Forge-Warden Tessika Ironmantle** — Seat of War
- Five additional members (identities not publicly disclosed)

## History

The Council formed in the final years of [The Sundering](../lore/the-sundering.md) when disparate factions needed unified command to survive. After [The Sundering](../lore/the-sundering.md) ended, the wartime coalition simply never dissolved — its members found they preferred ruling to returning to civilian life.

## Goals

- Maintain [Ironhold](../locations/ironhold.md)'s military and economic dominance in the Ashlands
- Control access to volcanic iron and the arcane resources of the caldera
- Suppress knowledge of pre-Sundering artifacts that might destabilize their authority
