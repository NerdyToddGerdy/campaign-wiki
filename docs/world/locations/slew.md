## Overview

Slew is a trade city built around commerce and the powerful Merchant Princes who control it. The city sits at the edge of a vast frozen swamp and serves as the hub for northern trade routes, including the road to the gem-trading villages beyond the wetlands. In Slew, economic power and political power are functionally the same thing.

## Notable Features

- **The Frozen Swamp**: The wetlands surrounding Slew freeze solid in winter, creating stable trade roads where none would otherwise exist. The city's prosperity depends on these seasonal routes remaining passable.
- **The Merchant Princes**: A council of wealthy traders who collectively govern Slew's commerce and, by extension, the city itself. Individual Princes control specific routes and contracts.
