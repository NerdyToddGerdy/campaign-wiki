## Overview

Ironhold is a fortified city carved into the volcanic basalt cliffs of the Ashlands. Its streets smell of forge-smoke and sulfur, and the eternal glow of the caldera below lights the lower districts at night. The city has served as the seat of [The Ember Council](../factions/the-ember-council.md) for over two centuries.

## Districts

**The Crucible** — The industrial heart of Ironhold. Smiths, alchemists, and artificers crowd its narrow streets. The heat here is oppressive year-round.

**The Ashward** — Residential quarters built into the cliff face itself. Families of miners and soldiers have lived here for generations.

**The Citadel** — A fortress-palace overlooking the city. Home to [The Ember Council](../factions/the-ember-council.md)'s chambers and the personal tower of [Malachar the Grey](../npcs/malachar-the-grey.md).

## History

Ironhold was founded by dwarven miners who struck an enormous vein of volcanic iron beneath the Ashlands plateau. Within a century, humans, tieflings, and genasi flocked to the city's forges. [The Ember Council](../factions/the-ember-council.md) rose to power during [The Sundering](../lore/the-sundering.md), positioning Ironhold as a refuge and military bulwark.

## Notable Figures

- **[Malachar the Grey](../npcs/malachar-the-grey.md)** — Archmage and senior Council member, resident of the Citadel's highest tower
- **Forge-Warden Tessika Ironmantle** — Commander of Ironhold's military garrison
