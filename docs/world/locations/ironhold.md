## Overview

Ironhold is a fortified city carved into the volcanic basalt cliffs of the Ashlands. Its streets smell of forge-smoke and sulfur, and the eternal glow of the caldera below lights the lower districts at night. The city has served as the seat of The Ember Council for over two centuries.

## Districts

**The Crucible** — The industrial heart of Ironhold. Smiths, alchemists, and artificers crowd its narrow streets. The heat here is oppressive year-round.

**The Ashward** — Residential quarters built into the cliff face itself. Families of miners and soldiers have lived here for generations.

**The Citadel** — A fortress-palace overlooking the city. Home to the Ember Council's chambers and the personal tower of Malachar the Grey.

## History

Ironhold was founded by dwarven miners who struck an enormous vein of volcanic iron beneath the Ashlands plateau. Within a century, humans, tieflings, and genasi flocked to the city's forges. The Ember Council rose to power during the Sundering, positioning Ironhold as a refuge and military bulwark.

## Notable Figures

- **Malachar the Grey** — Archmage and senior Council member, resident of the Citadel's highest tower
- **Forge-Warden Tessika Ironmantle** — Commander of Ironhold's military garrison
