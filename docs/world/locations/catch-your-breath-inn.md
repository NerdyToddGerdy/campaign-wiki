## Overview

The Catch Your Breath Inn is a well-known gathering place in Slew for hired swords, wandering clerics, and the otherwise unemployed. It has the comfortable reputation of a place where no one asks too many questions and the work tends to find you if you wait long enough.

## Known Facts

- Rivnee had been waiting there for over a week before the party assembled
- N'ail was directed to the party's corner table by the barkeep, Maria Heathertoes
- Merchant Prince Jorvan Eltharion approached the group here with a contract
