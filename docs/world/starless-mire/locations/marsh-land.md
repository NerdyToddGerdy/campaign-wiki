## Overview

Marsh Land is a small village deep in the Starless Mire, south of [Slew](../../northern-wetlands/locations/slew.md). It sits along a trade route connected to [Slew](../../northern-wetlands/locations/slew.md) and depends on that route for commerce and contact with the outside world.

## Notable Features

- Located inside the Starless Mire, well south of [Slew](../../northern-wetlands/locations/slew.md)
- Maintains trade ties with [Slew](../../northern-wetlands/locations/slew.md) via caravan

## Notable Figures

- **[Cathba](../../../keys-of-wonder/party/cathba.md)** — Shifter druid who was sent from Marsh Land to investigate a missing caravan

## Connections

- Trade route runs north to [Slew](../../northern-wetlands/locations/slew.md)
