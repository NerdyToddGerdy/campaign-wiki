## Description

The Staff of Embers is a six-foot rod of volcanic iron from the Ironhold caldera, its surface etched with runes that glow like dying coals. At its head, a crystal of solidified phoenix-fire contains a perpetual ember that has never gone cold in two centuries. It radiates warmth even in the deepest cold.

## Known Properties

- Grants the wielder mastery over fire magic, amplifying flame-based spells significantly
- The ember at its head grows brighter in the presence of divine magic or pre-Sundering artifacts
- Seems to respond to emotional states — the runes flare when its wielder is under threat

## History

The staff was forged during the Sundering by the mages of Ironhold as a symbol of the city's commitment to survival. It has been associated with The Ember Council ever since, though its exact possession has changed hands several times. Malachar the Grey is believed to keep it in his tower.

## Legends

Some texts suggest the staff was not created by mortal smiths at all — that it was a gift from Solara herself before her withdrawal, and that its core contains the last true divine flame still burning in the material world.

## Connections

- Associated with The Ember Council and Ironhold
- Malachar the Grey is its current reputed owner
- Referenced in academic texts about the Sundering
