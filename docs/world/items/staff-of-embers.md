## Description

The Staff of Embers is a six-foot rod of volcanic iron from the [Ironhold](../locations/ironhold.md) caldera, its surface etched with runes that glow like dying coals. At its head, a crystal of solidified phoenix-fire contains a perpetual ember that has never gone cold in two centuries. It radiates warmth even in the deepest cold.

## Known Properties

- Grants the wielder mastery over fire magic, amplifying flame-based spells significantly
- The ember at its head grows brighter in the presence of divine magic or pre-Sundering artifacts
- Seems to respond to emotional states — the runes flare when its wielder is under threat

## History

The staff was forged during [The Sundering](../lore/the-sundering.md) by the mages of [Ironhold](../locations/ironhold.md) as a symbol of the city's commitment to survival. It has been associated with [The Ember Council](../factions/the-ember-council.md) ever since, though its exact possession has changed hands several times. [Malachar the Grey](../npcs/malachar-the-grey.md) is believed to keep it in his tower.

## Legends

Some texts suggest the staff was not created by mortal smiths at all — that it was a gift from [Solara](../deities/solara.md) herself before her withdrawal, and that its core contains the last true divine flame still burning in the material world.
