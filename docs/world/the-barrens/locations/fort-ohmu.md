## Overview

The third and smallest of the Outpost Forts, Fort Ohmu was built from hardened clay and dust — fastened together by a powerful Transmuter from the [Alabaster Academy](../../general/locations/alabaster-academy.md). It sits at the eastern end of the border chain, on the edge of the Asharu Desert, and suffers brutal conditions: intense seasonal windstorms sweeping in from the dunes keep soldiers inside for much of the year.

## Notable Features

- **Desert Construction**: Unlike the stone forts to the west, Fort Ohmu was shaped from clay and desert materials by Academy magic. Its construction reflects the scarcity of the region.
- **Windstorm Season**: Intense seasonal storms from the Asharu Desert regularly pin soldiers indoors, making patrols and responses difficult for extended stretches.

## Notable Figures

- **Utilis Graal** *(deceased)* — Former commander. Inexplicably leaped from the top tower of the fort. His death has never been fully explained.

## Connections

- Easternmost of the three Outpost Forts; the others are [Fort Pratchett](fort-pratchett.md) (west) and [Fort Prefect](fort-prefect.md) (center)
- Sits on the edge of the Asharu Desert
- Constructed with assistance from the [Alabaster Academy](../../general/locations/alabaster-academy.md)

## Recent Developments

Morale has deteriorated since the commander's death, made worse by a replacement widely despised by the troops. The fort is undermanned. Nomad tribes have reported sightings of small humanoid creatures with jackal-like faces moving west across the desert — harassing the few humans who call the region home. Windstorms have so far prevented any effective response.
