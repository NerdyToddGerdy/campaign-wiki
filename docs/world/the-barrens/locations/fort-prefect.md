## Overview

The second of the three Outpost Forts, Fort Prefect serves a dual role: watchtower on the southern border and dungeon for captured interlopers from the Barrens. It sits between [Fort Pratchett](fort-pratchett.md) to the west and [Fort Ohmu](fort-ohmu.md) to the east, part of the chain of outposts that protects the Greenwold from monster incursions out of the Barrens.

## Notable Features

- **The Watchtower**: Provides sightlines across a broad stretch of the southern border.
- **The Dungeon**: Captured creatures and interlopers from the Barrens are held here.
- **Road Connection**: Part of the road running east from [Slew](../../northern-wetlands/locations/slew.md) through all three forts.

## Notable Figures

- **Marshall Jerrus Toley** — Commander. Still bears the scars of the Sorcerer's Rebellion — an uprising of unlicensed mages against the [Alabaster Academy](../../general/locations/alabaster-academy.md). His stubborn distrust of arcane magic and spellcasters has strained relations with the Academy and with magic-users throughout the region. He regards any magic-user as a potential agent or spy.

## Connections

- Second of the three Outpost Forts; flanked by [Fort Pratchett](fort-pratchett.md) (west) and [Fort Ohmu](fort-ohmu.md) (east)
- Part of the road supply chain running east from [Slew](../../northern-wetlands/locations/slew.md)
- Reinforcements can be dispatched quickly from [Thile](../../general/locations/thile.md) by riverboat in emergencies

## Recent Developments

A series of unexplained killings within the soldiers' ranks has prompted Marshall Toley to launch an investigation. Those within the fort regard it as more of an inquisition, given Toley's well-known suspicion of anything magical.
