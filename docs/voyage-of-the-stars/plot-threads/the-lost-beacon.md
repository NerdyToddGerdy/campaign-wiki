## Overview

Something has gone dark in the deep water — a presence or structure known to certain scholars and sea-priests as *the Lost Beacon*. The tide-caller Pibble Wavewatch risked her life to bring this warning to shore.

## Known Facts

- The Beacon's location is somewhere in the open sea, west of Saltmere
- It appears in pre-Sundering charts as a navigation point, then vanishes from records entirely
- Pibble's journal contains a partial location encoded in her tide-reading notations
- Whatever the Beacon is, its failure seems to correlate with something moving in the deep

## Leads

1. **Pibble Wavewatch** — Recovering in Saltmere. Knows more about the Beacon's nature than she's admitted.
2. **The Patron's Chest** — The locked chest the party delivered may be connected. Their contact never showed.
3. **Saltmere's Harbormaster** — Old enough to remember rumors about the Beacon from his father's time.
4. **[The Ember Council](../../world/factions/the-ember-council.md)** — Documents in [Malachar the Grey](../../world/npcs/malachar-the-grey.md)'s published works mention a "deep-water sentinel" without elaboration.

## Complications

- The party is being watched, and whoever is watching them was also watching the drop point.
- Pibble may not be a neutral party — her tide-caller order has its own stake in the Beacon.

## Status

**Active.** The party has the journal and knows the Beacon exists. They have not yet determined what it is or how urgent its failure may be.
