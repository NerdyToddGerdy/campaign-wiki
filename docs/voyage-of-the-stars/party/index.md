## The Crew of the Wandering Tern

The party of Voyage of the Stars travels aboard a modest sailing vessel, the *Wandering Tern*, working as couriers, investigators, and occasional trouble-solvers for a patron whose identity remains unclear.

## The Wandering Tern

A mid-sized caravel with faded blue sails and a figurehead carved as a wide-eyed seabird. The ship is paid for — though no one is entirely sure by whom. She's fast, sturdy, and has a hidden cargo hold beneath the main one.

---

## Party Notes

- The group has been working together for approximately six months before the campaign begins.
- They have a standing arrangement with a patron who communicates via sealed letters delivered to port offices.
- None of them have met the patron face-to-face.
