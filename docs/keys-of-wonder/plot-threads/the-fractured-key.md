## Overview

The party possesses one shard of a shattered artifact known — in the few texts that mention it — as the Fractured Key. The key, when complete, is said to open any lock. The shard they carry already demonstrates unusual properties, and it is clear others are searching for it.

## Known Facts

- The shard was sold by Aldus the Tinker at Coppergate Market in Verenmoor
- It resonates and points toward other shards in proximity
- A doorless building in Verenmoor's old quarter has a keyhole that matches the shard's shape
- The key appears in pre-Sundering texts as a sealed artifact of significant power

## Leads

1. **Aldus the Tinker** — Disappeared the morning after the sale. His calling card (a blank copper rectangle) still warms when held.
2. **The Doorless Building** — What's inside? Who sealed it? Why does the shard react to it?
3. **Pre-Sundering Libraries** — Verenmoor's old collegium library has restricted archives from before the Sundering.
4. **The Ember Council** — A vault beneath Ironhold's citadel reportedly contains records of every major artifact from the pre-Sundering era.

## Factions Interested in the Key

- **Unknown pursuers** — Someone was watching Aldus's stall. They did not bid.
- **The Ember Council** — May have records, or may want the shard actively.
- **Aldus's order** — The tinker is not what he seemed; his "order" or patron is unknown.

## Status

**Active.** The party has one shard. The resonance at Coppergate suggests at least two more are nearby. The doorless building awaits.
